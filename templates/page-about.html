Dossier · Who I Am

ABOUT

I grew up in Buenos Aires surrounded by sound — the kind of city where music bleeds through every wall and every conversation has a rhythm to it. That's where I built Panacea, my recording studio, in 2015. I ran the technical side, led the production team, and shaped the sonic identity of every project that came through the door. Eventually I brought that same intensity to the U.S., earning a Bachelor of Science in Music Production from Full Sail University, where I graduated as Valedictorian with the Advanced Achiever award.

Today I'm finishing an MBA in Applied AI in Business at Westcliff University while working as a freelance producer and engineer for artists and labels across the Americas. That same discipline I bring to music — building systems, solving problems, making things work — now extends into creative business consulting and AI workflow integration for studios, labels, and agencies.

20+ years, 50+ collaborations, and more hours behind the console than I can count — I've learned that great sound isn't just technical. It's knowing when to push, when to pull back, and when to let the silence do the work. I'm also a voting member of both The Recording Academy and The Latin Recording Academy, because the craft deserves to be taken seriously at every level.

Education & Mentorship

WHAT I KNOW, I PASS ON.

The gap between what audio programs teach and what the studio actually demands is something I think about a lot. Theory gets you in the door — but session management, client communication, knowing when a take is the one before the artist does — that's all earned behind the glass. I mentor emerging producers and engineers to bridge that gap faster than I did.

That means mix critiques with context, not just "turn up the vocals." It means understanding how to build a client base from zero, how to price your work, how to run a session when nothing is going according to plan. The kind of knowledge that doesn't fit in a curriculum — but makes the difference between someone who knows the tools and someone who can deliver under pressure.
